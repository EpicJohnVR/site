About John

John Fiore, more commonly known as EpicJohn, is a 14 year old Gorilla Tag and VR Gaming Content Creator. His content ranges from Gorilla Tag, Rec Room VR, Garry's Mod, and other VR and/or PC games. John is also an Indie Game Developer, using the Unity Game Engine, and formally Construct 3. He often films by himself, but will also film with other Content Creators and Friends. This includes: Zerdy, Juan, Nutty Buddy, TNT_2020, etc. He has a friend group and filming group called "El Groupo", which consists of himself and 3 other content creators: Juan, Zerdy, and Gepo.
